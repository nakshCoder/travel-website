About The Oberoi Cecil

Stay at a heritage property with glowing fireplaces and rich wooden floors and enjoy views of
majestic mountains.

  • Pamper yourself with soothing body treatments and therapies at the signature spa at The Oberoi Cecil, Shimla.

  • Explore Shimla's history and forested trails with guided heritage walks and treks.

  • Savour a lavish international and regional fare at the hotel's exquisite restaurant.

  • Let your kids have fun in the activity room with interesting board games, an Xbox gaming system and more.
